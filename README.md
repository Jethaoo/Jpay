# Jpay

Jpay is a mobile app for tracking shared expenses with friends in one place. It
keeps your ledgers organized, shows who owes what at a glance, and makes it easy
to stay on top of group spending without messy spreadsheets.

## What It Does
- **Ledger creation**: Start a ledger for a trip, roommates, or any shared spend so everything stays in one place.
- **Dashboard summary**: See total outstanding balances and how many ledgers are active at a glance.
- **Friends per ledger**: Track who is involved in each ledger to keep group expenses organized.
- **Recent activity view**: Quickly find and open your most recent ledgers.
- **Profile management**: Update your display name and add a profile photo.

## Why It Exists
Splitting expenses can be awkward and easy to lose track of. Jpay focuses on
clarity and quick updates so you can spend less time reconciling and more time
enjoying the plan.

## Tech Stack
- Flutter (Material 3)
- Firebase Auth
- Cloud Firestore
- Firebase Storage
